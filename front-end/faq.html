What IS Fake News?
Report Article
Fake News definition from: https://www.theglobeandmail.com/community/digital-lab/fake-news-quiz-how-to-spot/article33821986/

Fake news IS NOT: 

- Satire 

- Honest reporting mistakes

Fake news IS: 

- Disinformation for profit, crime, or political gain 

- Viral pranks or hoaxes

LegitiMate will not distinguish between satire and fake news. 

Our goal is to flag any sketchy articles so you can do some fact checking where more diligence is needed. 

When you report an article as fake news or report that a real news article is being flagged as fake news, our team will fack check the article against known reputable sources. 

The article will then be classfied into the appropriate category and fed into LegitiMate, making it even better at catching fake news!
